5. Data Storage and Security

Where is my data stored?
All your personal data (favorites, account info, download history) is stored locally on your device using your browser's localStorage. We do not have access to this data on any external server.

How is my data protected?
We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

What happens if I clear my browser data?
If you clear your browser's cache and localStorage, your account information and favorites will be permanently deleted.
